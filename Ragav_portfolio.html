  • Master’s in Business Analytics
    UCD, Dublin (2024–2025)

    Fairness in Sports Scheduling

    This project investigates the fairness of English Premier League (EPL) fixture schedules using historical match data. It analyzes key factors such as rest day differences, travel distances, opponent difficulty, and home/away sequencing to quantify scheduling bias. Through statistical analysis and simulation modeling, the study provides actionable insights and recommendations to improve competitive balance and scheduling transparency in professional football.
